FAQ

Clear answers before you start.

Is ClipCut Studio free?
Yes — completely free, no sign-up. Shortify gives you every caption style, animated captions, AI best-moment picking and unlimited use right in your browser, and the Bulk Downloader is a free Windows app.
Does my video get uploaded to a server?
No. Shortify runs entirely in your browser — your video never leaves your device.
Do I need an account?
No — there's no account or login. Just open Shortify and start making shorts.
Which platforms are supported?
Shorts work everywhere (TikTok, Reels, YouTube Shorts). The Downloader supports YouTube, TikTok, Instagram and Pinterest. The Uploader posts to Facebook Pages.
