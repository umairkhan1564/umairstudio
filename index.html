FAQ

Clear answers before you start.

Is ClipCut Studio free?
Yes — Shortify and the Bulk Downloader are free to use. A Pro tier with premium caption styles, face tracking, 1080p/2K and full-episode support is launching soon.
Does my video get uploaded to a server?
No. Shortify runs entirely in your browser — your video never leaves your device.
Do I need an account?
Browsing is open for everyone. To make shorts with Shortify you sign in with Google — one click, free.
Which platforms are supported?
Shorts work everywhere (TikTok, Reels, YouTube Shorts). The Downloader supports YouTube, TikTok, Instagram and Pinterest. The Uploader posts to Facebook Pages.
What's in the upcoming Pro plan?
Server-powered processing: full episodes (2GB+), face tracking, premium animated caption styles, 1080p/2K output, better accuracy for Urdu/Hindi, and priority rendering — ad-free.
