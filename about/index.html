About ClipCut Studio

ClipCut Studio builds free, practical tools for content creators. We run Facebook pages and make videos ourselves, so every tool here was built to solve a problem we actually had:

  • Shortify — because turning a long video into good shorts with captions took hours in an editor, and the popular tools put it behind a subscription.
  • Bulk Downloader — because collecting content for editing, one link at a time, was painful.
  • Bulk Uploader — because posting to multiple Facebook Pages every day shouldn't be a full-time job.

Our approach is different from most "AI tools" sites: wherever possible the work happens in your browser, on your device. That keeps the tools genuinely free, fast, and private — your videos never get uploaded anywhere.

The tools are free to use, supported by ads. A Pro tier with heavier server-powered features (full episodes, face tracking, premium caption styles) is on the way.
